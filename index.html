About Chief Pay & Accounts Office

The Chief Pay & Accounts Office (CPAO), Andaman & Nicobar Administration, is the principal accounts authority under the Government of India for the Union Territory of Andaman & Nicobar Islands. It functions under the overall supervision of the Financial Advisor & Chief Accounts Officer.

CPAO is responsible for pre-audit and payment of bills pertaining to all departments of the A&N Administration, maintenance of Government Provident Fund accounts, sanctioning of loans and advances to government servants, compilation of monthly accounts, and submission of returns to the Controller General of Accounts (CGA), New Delhi.

Functions

  • Pre-audit and payment of salary, pension, TA/DA, and contingency bills
  • Maintenance of GPF accounts for Group A, B & C employees
  • Sanction and disbursement of House Building Advance (HBA), Motor Car Advance (MCA), and other personal advances
  • Budget monitoring and expenditure control
  • Compilation of monthly civil accounts and submission to CGA
  • Internal audit and inspection of sub-offices and DDOs
  • Coordination with PAO offices for Central Sector Schemes
